About SweetCrumbs

Our Story

SweetCrumbs began when a group of four college students shared a passion for baking.
They spent the next four years developing the tastiest recipes with high-quality ingredients.
In 2026, they opened SweetCrumbs Bakery to share their love of treats with the whole world.
Now, anyone can stop by and experience the joy of sharing something sweet.
