Common Questions

Do you offer vegan or gluten-free options?

We have a rotating selection of vegan cookies and gluten-free brownies available daily. Just ask our staff what's fresh today!

How early should I order a custom cake?

We recommend placing custom orders at least 1 week in advance. For weddings or large events, 1 month is preferred.

Do you take pre-orders for holiday treats?

Yes, we open holiday pre-orders on our website two weeks before major holidays. Keep an eye on our homepage for updates.

Are your ingredients locally sourced?

Whenever possible! We source our dairy and seasonal fruits from local farms within the tristate area.
